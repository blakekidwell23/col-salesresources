The Rules
What counts as a "call"?
Any logged entry in HubSpot. LVM, NA, SPOKE — they all count. No log entry = no credit. Don't backfill after the fact.
How do I earn the $20 spiff?
Your HubSpot call log entry must exist before the customer's invoice date. Log the call first, then they order — that's your $20. After that, $5 for every additional order they place through Dec 31.
What if someone else takes an inbound from my account?
That's normal customer service — serve them well. But it doesn't earn you the spiff and it doesn't earn the other rep the spiff either. The bounty list is locked by assignment.
Do converted accounts stay on my list?
Yes. Once a customer converts, they stay assigned to you for the rest of 2026. All their revenue counts toward your leaderboard total — so the earlier you convert them, the more revenue you accumulate.
What if I'm out sick or on PTO?
PTO and sick days are pro-rated out of your activity minimum. Let your manager know and they'll adjust your target accordingly.
Is the list fair? Did someone get easier accounts?
Yes — every rep has the same tier mix within their region. Accounts were snake-drafted by quality score to make sure no one got stacked with all cold leads. Check the Fairness Audit tab if you're curious.
